### Scoring
  * Starring a practice: 1 point
  * Cocontribution: 
    * initial points:
      * major: 1 point
      * minor: 0.5 points
      * patch: 0.1 points
    * subsequent stars
      * Sum(initial points)
      * Ie. if a user adds 2 major contributions, and 1 minor contribution, each successive occasion a new user stars the practice results in the cocontributor receiving 2.5 points
